What is VetriVel Farmings?

An innovative agricultural leader integrating advanced technology with timeless farming traditions to cultivate pristine, organic coconut products directly from our resilient soil.

How We Differ

Wisdom, Work, and Worship — shaping innovations that preserve culture while embracing global growth.

VetriVel Farms stands as a testament to his vision of merging modern intelligence with the timeless purity of nature, ensuring that growth makes a positive impact on the soil and the lives of those who till it.

Raw Purity

Coconut & Alevior

We specialize in agricultural harvesting, cultivating pristine organic coconuts and pure Alevior. We supply these foundational, raw botanicals directly to premium extraction mills and eco-conscious manufacturers worldwide.

Farm-Fresh Coconuts

touch_app

Farm-Fresh Coconuts

Cultivated in our nutrient-rich soil, our organic coconuts are carefully hand-harvested to ensure maximum freshness and quality. We provide pure, raw coconut yields consistently at scale for the finest extraction mills and global markets.

Pure Alevior Yields

touch_app

Pure Alevior Yields

We grow pristine, high-grade Alevior (Aloe Vera), harvesting the pure botanical leaves to deliver unadulterated, raw ingredients. We empower wellness and cosmetic partners with nature's truest healing properties directly from the source.

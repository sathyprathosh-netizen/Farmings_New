The Root of Purity

Our Story

Where tradition meets technology. For over three decades, we have cultivated the highlands of Tamil Nadu, turning fertile red soil into a legacy of purity.

VetriVel Farms is the ethical engine driving our commercial success. We believe that genuine purity starts at the source — ensuring superior quality oil and cosmetics while nourishing the land and the communities that cultivate it.

The Visionary Behind

Engr. Vetri Vel Sathya Raj

Leading the VetriVel Group with a commitment to ethics, innovation, and sustainable global impact. Engr. Vetri Vel Sathya Raj is the Founder & CEO of the VetriVel Group of Companies, integrating advanced technologies with traditional values.

"Wisdom, Work, and Worship — shaping innovations that preserve culture while embracing global growth."

VetriVel Farms stands as a testament to his vision of merging modern intelligence with the timeless purity of nature, ensuring that growth makes a positive impact on the soil and the lives of those who till it.

The Farming Process

Where the land remembers every season

Our farmers work in harmony with the natural rhythm of the Tamil Nadu highlands. Each coconut tree is cultivated over decades — carefully tended, never rushed — to produce fruit of extraordinary purity.

  • 01
    Soil Preparation

    Rich red soil, enriched with organic compost and balanced naturally.

  • 02
    Sustainable Irrigation

    Drip irrigation systems conserve water while nourishing deep roots.

  • 03
    Hand Harvesting

    Each coconut is hand-picked at perfect ripeness — never too early.
